This section contains information, related to converting TX ID's to *0 format. If you cannot find an answer to your question, shoot me an email - ryanstefan@protectamerica.com.

What does it convert?
The ID converter will sift through any 6 character string; e.g. all numbers: 123456, letters and numbers: 1b3d5f and all letters: abcdef. Anything that is all letters will be displayed into a choice box to the right (anything not a word is almost certainly a TX ID). Choices will be added to the main conversion in it's original order.
Why do I have too many ID’s?
The zones archive page will sometimes have 6 character long numbers that are not TX ID’s. If this happens, I recommend just adding another number to the end of each (its quicker than deleting them manually). That way, the converter will simply ignore them.
