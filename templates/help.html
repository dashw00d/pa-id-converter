Why do I have too many ID’s
The zones archive page will sometimes have 6 character long numbers that are not TX ID’s. If this happens, I recommend just adding another number to the end of each (its quicker than deleted them manually). That way, the converter will ignore them.
What does it convert?
The ID converter will sift through any 6 character string; e.g. all numbers: 123456, letters and numbers: 1b3d5f and all letters: abcdef. Anything that is all letters will be displayed into a selection box.
